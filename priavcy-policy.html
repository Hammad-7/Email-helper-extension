Privacy Policy for Email Writer Extension

Last updated: May 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Email Writer Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension collects and processes the following information:

  • Email Content: When you use our extension, we temporarily access the content of your currently open email to generate appropriate response suggestions.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as your preferred response tone and style are stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide our core service of generating contextually relevant email responses
  • To improve and optimize the performance of our extension
  • To remember your preferences between sessions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your email content is only processed temporarily and is not stored beyond what is necessary to generate responses. 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our service or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we process about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Permissions

The Email Writer Extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To run scripts only on user interaction (e.g., clicking the extension icon).
  • https://mail.google.com/*: To read open emails in Gmail to generate responses.
  • storage: To remember user preferences locally.
